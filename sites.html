Candidate Sites — Lebanon WWTP Site Selection

Lebanon WWTP Site Selection — Public Record Archive  |  Return to main page

The following sites were under evaluation by the City of Lebanon Site Selection Committee as of May 31, 2026. Highlighted sites (green) are within or immediately adjacent to the city limits of Lebanon — the alternatives the petitioners are requesting the committee prioritize. All other sites are outside the city limits and are the subject of the public record statements on this site; Sites 1–7 form the primary rural residential corridor along Coles Ferry Pike, Mann Road, and Berea Church Road, with Sites 8–12, 18, and 19 sharing the same core concerns. The committee added sites 13A and 16–19 on June 2, 2026; Rev C of the public record statement acknowledges nineteen sites. Site 13A brings the full list to twenty.

Candidate Sites — Lebanon WWTP Site Selection

Lebanon WWTP Site Selection — Public Record Archive  |  Return to main page

The following sites were under evaluation by the City of Lebanon Site Selection Committee as of May 31, 2026. Sites 1–7 (highlighted) fall within the rural residential corridor along Coles Ferry Pike, Mann Road, and Berea Church Road that is the subject of the public record statements on this site. The committee added sites 13A and 16–19 on June 2, 2026; Rev C of the public record statement acknowledges nineteen sites. Site 13A brings the full list to twenty.
